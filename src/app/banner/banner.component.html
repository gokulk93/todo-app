How to use

The purpose of ideasGo is to manage all your personal tasks or day-to-day activities or goals or ideas which gonna put into effect and track its status in one place.

  • To add a new task you can add them through Add Task text box. It will be automatically queued up to the New Tasks box
  • To move a task to in progress or active, simply Drag and Drop them to Progressive box. Which indicates that the task is a work in progress
  • To move a task to completed, Drag and Drop them to Completed box. which indicates that the task has been completed
  • IdeasGo will automaticaly update the chart based on your activities
  • Quick notes - As its name referes it allows you to store any quick info or notes and store it in your browser cookies
  • All these data are avaialble in your browser cookies. It helps to store your data until browser cookies got cleared
  • IdeasGo allows you to download and upload all your tasks in json file, under the Features tab
  • Clear Data under Features tab help to wipe off all your data
